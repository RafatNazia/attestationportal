FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

How long does document attestation take?
+
Standard attestation takes 5–7 working days. Express service available in 3 days for most documents. Timeline varies by country and document type.
What documents do I need to submit?
+
Original document + photocopy, valid passport copy, and a filled inquiry form. Additional documents may be required based on the destination country and document type.
Is my document safe with you?
+
Absolutely. We use secure, insured courier services and maintain strict document handling protocols. All documents are tracked from pickup to delivery with ID-verified handover.
What is the difference between Attestation and Apostille?
+
Attestation is for non-Hague convention countries (UAE, Saudi, Qatar etc.) requiring Embassy stamp. Apostille is for 120+ Hague Convention member countries (USA, UK, Germany etc.) — it's a single international stamp by MEA.
Do you offer home pickup and delivery?
+
Yes! We provide doorstep pickup and delivery across Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kochi, and 50+ other cities across India.
